kalaradata

an open data project about the education economy in zelecaf

objectives

  1. publish a map of all the actors of this economy on the internet

stategy

  1. identify all major private and public actors by country - due date: feb 15 2021
  2. describe all the personna involves in this economy - due date: mar 15 2021
  3. publish a platform on the internet where actors can share their needs and services - due date: apr 15 2021
  4. identify all the products and services availables in the education ecenomy in zelecaf - due date: may 15 2021
  5. build a map of actors products and services and publish it on the internet - due date: jun 15 2021

means

we will use economic intelligence to gather data on the internet

measures

  1. number of actors identified
  2. number of products identified
  3. number of services identified
